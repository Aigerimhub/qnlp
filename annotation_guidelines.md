# Annotation Guidelines

## Annotation Unit

Each instance contains a query or case description, one or more relevant evidence clauses, and one final decision label.

## Decision Labels

### Eligible
Assign `Eligible` when all mandatory conditions are supported and no disqualifying condition is present.

### Not Eligible
Assign `Not Eligible` when at least one mandatory condition is explicitly violated or a disqualifying rule applies.

### Needs Review
Assign `Needs Review` when the evidence is incomplete, ambiguous, contradictory, or dependent on unavailable information.

## Evidence Selection Rules

Select a clause only when it directly states an applicable condition, defines an exception/threshold/deadline/procedure, is required to justify the final label, or adds nonredundant information.
